About Me

A classically trained soprano, Ramsey has studied under Karen Nimereala, Franco Corelli's pupil, and sings in English, Spanish, Italian, French, Hebrew, and more. Her fascination with languages and the voice led her to spend time studying different styles of folksong, dance, and martial arts in NYC and around the world.
